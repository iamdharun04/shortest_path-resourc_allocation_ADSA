About Us

Gear Planner is a web application designed to facilitate the planning of backpacking trips, catering to outdoor enthusiasts seeking to optimize their gear selection based on specific trip parameters. The application employs an efficient knapsack algorithm to recommend essential gear items tailored to factors such as trip duration, weather conditions, terrain type, and weight constraints. Developed by a team with a shared passion for hiking, camping, and technology, Gear Planner aims to streamline the packing process, ensuring users are well-equipped for their outdoor adventures without unnecessary weight or oversight.
