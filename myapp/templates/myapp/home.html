Data Structures Used

Knapsack Algorithm:

The knapsack algorithm is a combinatorial optimization problem where the goal is to maximize the total value of items in a knapsack without exceeding its weight capacity. It's commonly used in resource allocation, budgeting, and scheduling problems.

Prim's Algorithm:

Prim's algorithm is used to find the minimum spanning tree (MST) of a connected weighted graph. It starts from an arbitrary node and repeatedly adds the minimum weight edge that connects a vertex from the tree to a vertex outside of the tree until all vertices are included in the MST.
